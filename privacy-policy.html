Section 01

About Us

Prayudhi Strategic Consulting Pty Ltd (ABN: 36 675 629 071 | ACN: 675 629 071) is a holding company incorporated in Australia, operating cross-border services between Australia and Indonesia. Our registered business units include:

  • WanderOz Tours — Curated travel and tour experiences across Australia for individuals, groups, and corporate clients.
  • WonderOz JasTip — Cross-border personal shopping (jasa titip) and delivery services between Australia and Indonesia.
  • WondrOz Digital — Digital solutions including social media management, website development, and branding for cross-border businesses.

References to "Prayudhi", "we", "our", or "us" in this Privacy Policy refer to Prayudhi Strategic Consulting Pty Ltd and all of its business units collectively, unless otherwise specified.

Section 03

How We Collect Information

We collect personal information in the following ways:

  • Directly from you — when you contact us via email, WhatsApp, or our website contact form; when you place an order or book a service; when you provide documentation for travel or customs purposes.
  • Automatically — through cookies and analytics tools when you visit our websites (see Section 8).
  • From third parties — including payment processors, travel partners, logistics providers, and Australian or Indonesian customs authorities where required by law.
  • From publicly available sources — such as business registration records, LinkedIn, or public social media profiles, where relevant to our services.

We will always notify you at the time of collection regarding the purpose for which your information is being collected, where it is not already obvious from the context of your interaction with us.

Section 05

Disclosure of Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers — Third-party vendors who assist us in delivering our services, including payment processors, shipping and logistics companies, travel operators, IT service providers, and cloud hosting platforms. These parties are contractually bound to handle your data securely and only for the purpose for which it was shared.
  • Customs and Border Authorities — For WonderOz JasTip orders, personal information and item details may be shared with the Directorate General of Customs and Excise (Bea Cukai) of Indonesia and the Australian Border Force as required by applicable import/export law.
  • Travel Partners — For WanderOz Tours bookings, relevant personal details may be shared with airlines, accommodation providers, transport companies, and tour operators to fulfil your travel arrangement.
  • Legal Obligations — We may disclose information where required by law, court order, or at the direction of a regulatory authority in Australia or Indonesia.
  • Business Transfers — In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your personal information may be transferred to the relevant successor entity, subject to equivalent privacy protections.
  • With Your Consent — For any other disclosure not listed above, we will obtain your explicit consent prior to sharing your information.
Section 06

Cross-Border Data Transfers

As a cross-border business operating between Australia and Indonesia, the transfer of personal information between the two countries is fundamental to how our services operate. By engaging our services, you acknowledge and consent to the transfer of your personal information between Australia and Indonesia.

We take reasonable steps to ensure that overseas recipients of your personal information are subject to privacy obligations that are substantially similar to those applicable in Australia under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth), consistent with Australian Privacy Principle 8 (APP 8).

For Indonesian customers, we comply with the provisions of UU No. 27 Tahun 2022 (Personal Data Protection Law) with respect to cross-border data transfers, including ensuring an adequate level of protection in the receiving country or obtaining your explicit consent where required.

Your data may be stored and processed in cloud infrastructure located in Australia, Singapore, or the United States, depending on the service provider used. We ensure all such providers maintain appropriate security certifications and data protection standards.

Section 07

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law. Our general retention periods are as follows:

Data Type Retention Period
Customer transaction records 7 years (in accordance with Australian tax law)
Customs and import/export documentation 5 years (or as required by Indonesian customs law)
Travel booking records and documentation 3 years after completion of travel
Marketing communications & consent records Until withdrawal of consent or 3 years of inactivity
General correspondence 2 years from last interaction
Website analytics data 26 months (anonymised after 14 months)

Upon expiry of the applicable retention period, personal information will be securely deleted or de-identified so that it can no longer be attributed to you as an individual.

Section 08

Our websites use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your browsing experience, analyse website traffic, and understand how visitors interact with our content.

Section 09

Security of Your Information

We take the security of your personal information seriously. We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission using SSL/TLS protocols on all websites
  • Access controls and authentication requirements for internal systems
  • Regular review of our information security practices
  • Staff training on privacy and data handling obligations
  • Secure document storage and restricted physical access where applicable

While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to take reasonable precautions to protect your own personal information when communicating with us online.

In the event of a data breach that is likely to result in serious harm, we will notify affected individuals and, where required, the relevant regulatory authority (such as the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner) in accordance with the Notifiable Data Breaches (NDB) scheme under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

Section 10

Your Rights

Depending on your country of residence, you have certain rights in relation to the personal information we hold about you. These may include:

10.1 Rights for Australian Residents

  • Access — You may request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction — You may request that we correct personal information that is inaccurate, out-of-date, incomplete, irrelevant, or misleading.
  • Complaint — You may lodge a complaint with us if you believe we have breached your privacy. If unsatisfied with our response, you may escalate your complaint to the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at www.oaic.gov.au.

10.2 Rights for Indonesian Residents

Under UU No. 27 Tahun 2022, Indonesian residents have the following rights:

  • Right to Access — Request access to your personal data held by us.
  • Right to Correction — Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to Deletion — Request deletion of your personal data where it is no longer needed for the purpose for which it was collected, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent — Withdraw your consent for processing at any time, where processing is based on consent.
  • Right to Object — Object to the processing of your personal data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Data Portability — Request that we provide your personal data in a structured, commonly used format where technically feasible.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 14 below. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe, and in any case within 30 days.

Section 11

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors without the explicit consent of a parent or legal guardian.

For WanderOz Tours bookings that include minors as travellers, we collect necessary travel-related information (such as passport details) as provided by the parent or guardian who is responsible for the booking. Such information is used solely for the purpose of fulfilling the travel arrangement.

If you believe that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a minor without appropriate consent, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete that information promptly.
